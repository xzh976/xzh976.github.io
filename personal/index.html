Biography

I am a Ph. D. student in Victoria University of Wellington (VUW), supervised by Dr. Fang-Lue Zhang, Prof. Paul Rosin and Prof. Yu-Kun Lai. My current research interests are image/video processing and generation. In 2022, I received my master degree in the Sichuan University (Project 985 in China), supervised by Pro. Wei Li, studying deep learning, image/ video object detection and image video processing.

During the period 2022 to 2023, I have worked in CUHK (SZ) and Westlake University as a Research Assistant, supervised by Prof. Qilin Sun and Prof. Xin Yuan. I studied Computational photography and Video Snapshot Compressive Imaging.

Research Interests: Deep learning, Object detection, Computational photogtaphy, Video/ image processing, Video/ image generation

Skills: Pytorch, Python, Matlab, Protus, AutoCAD, PS, PR, Procreat
