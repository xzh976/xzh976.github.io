Biography

I am a Ph. D. student in Victoria University of Wellington (VUW), supervised by Dr. Fang-Lue Zhang, Prof. Yu-Kun Lai, and Prof. Paul Rosin. In 2022, I received my master degree in the Sichuan University (Project 985 in China), supervised by Prof. Wei Li.
During the period 2022 to 2023, I have worked in CUHK (SZ) and Westlake University as a Research Assistant, supervised by Prof. Qilin Sun and Prof. Xin Yuan.
My research interests are Computational Imaging, Image/Video Processing and Generation.
